Classes at True North Homeschool Academy

I will be teaching Russian 1 for True North Homeschool Academy this year, live on-line from Kentucky, US. These classes are designed for students ages 14-21 and are geared towards language enthusiasts wanting to learn basic Russian communication, foundational reading skills and cultural awareness.

If you have a high school student who has little to no prior knowledge of the Russian language and who is looking to earn a HS Foreign Language Credit, my Russian 1 class is for them!
